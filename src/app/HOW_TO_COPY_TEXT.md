# 📋 HOW TO COPY TEXT - SUPER SIMPLE GUIDE

## The Copy Buttons Don't Work? No Problem! 👍

Your browser is blocking automatic copying. But copying manually is SUPER EASY:

---

## ✨ THE MAGIC TRIPLE-CLICK METHOD

### Step 1: Triple-Click 👆👆👆
Click **3 times fast** on any black box with text.

**What happens?** All the text gets selected automatically! (It will turn blue/highlighted)

### Step 2: Copy 📋
Press:
- **Windows/Linux:** `Ctrl + C`
- **Mac:** `Cmd + C`

### Step 3: Paste 📝
Go to your terminal and press:
- **Windows/Linux:** `Ctrl + V`
- **Mac:** `Cmd + V`

---

## 🎯 EXAMPLE:

You see this:
```
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐

└─────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Do this:**
1. 👆👆👆 Triple-click inside the black box
2. Text turns blue (selected!)
3. Press `Ctrl+C` or `Cmd+C`
4. Paste in terminal with `Ctrl+V` or `Cmd+V`

**DONE!** ✅

## 💡 PRO TIPS:

### Visual Cues:
- Black boxes will turn **green** when you hover = ready to copy!
- All black boxes are **selectable** - just click!

### Alternative Method:
If triple-click doesn't work:
1. Click once in the box
2. Press `Ctrl+A` (or `Cmd+A`) to select all
3. Press `Ctrl+C` (or `Cmd+C`) to copy

### Can't Select?
Try:
1. Click and drag across the text
2. Or click at the start, hold Shift, click at the end
3. Then `Ctrl+C` or `Cmd+C`
